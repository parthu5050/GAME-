Pah Tum Rules & Scoring Architecture

1. Grid & Holes

Played on 5x5 to 9x9 grids. Randomly blocked tiles ("black holes") are placed prior to the first move.

2. Placement

Players take turns placing one stone at a time on open tiles. Placed stones cannot be moved.

3. Line Points

Points awarded for uninterrupted straight lines (horiz/vert):
• 3 stones = 3 pts
• 4 stones = 10 pts
• 5 stones = 25 pts

4. Win Condition

Game ends when all playable tiles are filled. Player with highest point total wins the round.
