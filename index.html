Pah Tum Cyber-Arena

Full 3-mode knockout tournament engine (H v H, H v M, M v M). Featuring explicit Play/Pause state controls, strict 5s move timers, interactive knockout brackets, and instant simulation telemetry.

Pah Tum Rules & System Specifications

1. Grid & Holes

5x5 to 9x9 grids. Randomly blocked tiles ("black holes") are placed prior to initial stone placement.

2. 3 Game Modes

Support for Human vs Human (H v H), Human vs Machine (H v M), and Machine vs Machine (M v M Spectator).

3. Playback Bar & 5s Timer

Explicit Play, Pause, and Restart controls. Strict 5-second countdown forfeits turn if exceeded.

4. Knockout Telemetry

Full bracket tree advancement (QF to Finals) with match history logs and average move time analytics.
